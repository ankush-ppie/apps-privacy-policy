Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 26, 2025

App Name: Checkers and Draughts

Welcome

Thank you for choosing Checkers and Draughts. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information. Checkers and Draughts is designed as an offline game that respects your privacy.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Game statistics or scores
  • User-generated content
  • Usage analytics or behavior tracking data
  • Device information or system logs

However, please note that our advertising partners may collect certain information as described in the "Advertisements" section below.

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any information, we do not use, process, or analyze any personal data. Your gameplay experience remains entirely on your device.

Data Storage

All game data, including:

  • Game settings and preferences
  • Current game state
  • Player configurations

are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We do not collect or share your personal information. However, our advertising partners may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies as described in the "Advertisements" section. This data collection is handled directly by the advertising networks and is not controlled by us.

We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (though no personal data would be transferred as we don't collect any)

Third-Party Services

While Checkers and Draughts operates primarily offline, we integrate with third-party advertising networks to display ads that support our free game. These services may collect data as described in the "Advertisements" section. The app does not integrate with analytics platforms, social media platforms, or other third-party services beyond advertising.

Advertisements

Checkers and Draughts displays advertisements to support our free game. These ads are provided by third-party advertising networks that may collect certain information to show relevant advertisements.

Information collected by advertising partners may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage patterns
  • Approximate location (for location-based ads)

Popular advertising networks we may use include:

  • Google AdMob
  • Facebook Audience Network
  • Unity Ads
  • Other legitimate advertising partners

These advertising partners have their own privacy policies that govern how they collect and use data. We encourage you to review their privacy policies:

  • Google AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads Privacy Policy

Your choices regarding ads:

  • You may be able to opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • On Android devices: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • On iOS devices: Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising > Limit Ad Tracking

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal information, this app does not knowingly collect data from children of any age. The app operates entirely offline for gameplay purposes.

Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no security risks related to data breaches or unauthorized access to your information. All game data remains secure on your local device.

Your Rights

As we do not collect any personal information, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your device and any locally stored game preferences.

International Data Transfers

No data transfers occur as the app operates entirely offline without any data collection or transmission.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be reflected by updating the effective date. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically for any changes.
